## The 7 Modules

### 1. First Watch: Morning Planning That Actually Works

Start every day by precisely planning what truly matters, with the help of AI. First Watch helps you set clear goals and prioritize tasks — so you're not reacting to the day, but leading it.

### 2. Hourly GPS: Stay on Track Without Burning Out

Experience real-time optimization throughout your entire day. Hourly GPS provides quick check-ins that keep you on course — or flag deviations before they derail your schedule. Small adjustments, big results. And the best part: it doesn't require exhausting willpower. All you need to do is take a minute every now and then to check in. You'll know exactly where your time went and why.

### 3. Movement Tracker: Track What You Enjoy. See Your Progress.

Daily GPS started as an AI-assisted "swim log." The Movement Tracker keeps that spirit alive as an essential part of the system.

Use it to track what moves you and what you love doing. Log running, cycling, swimming, walking, yoga, creative activities, meditation, singing, or any other daily habit you want to maintain consistently.

Set a goal. Track your progress. Watch your momentum grow.

Your activity matters. And so does everything else. The Movement Tracker is a simple way to make sure your life doesn't revolve entirely around work.

### 4. The DIM System: Capture Ideas Without Losing Focus

Distractions, ideas, random tasks — they all need somewhere to land. The DIM System gives wandering thoughts, new ideas, and miscellaneous tasks a home. It lets you acknowledge them WITHOUT losing your focus. Capture now, process later. Stay on track.

The DIM System automatically analyzes, categorizes, and prioritizes ideas, then makes recommendations using the **4 D's**: *Do It, Defer It, Delegate It, or Delete It.* It's customizable from day 1 based on what matters most to you, and designed to adapt and evolve as you progress over time.

### 5. Priority Engine: Know What's Most Important. Fully Automatic.

Not every idea deserves your attention today. The Priority Engine uses AI to evaluate every idea you capture against filters like urgency, revenue impact, alignment, and effort. The system is designed so you can set your own filters from day one.

The Priority Engine then gives you recommendations using the **4 D's methodology**: Do it, Defer it, Delegate it, or Delete it.

No more decision fatigue. No more confusion about what to work on or when. The Priority Engine acts as an AI life and business coach — keeping your priorities clear and your focus sharp.

### 6. Night Watch: Recognize Your Patterns, Own Your Progress

Night Watch is an evening reflection that reveals what's truly working. It helps you identify peak performance moments and celebrate your wins.

It also surfaces hidden patterns where you may have drifted during the day, while providing course corrections so tomorrow's First Watch keeps you smoothly on track.

This is how the system gets to know YOU — and helps you become the best version of yourself, consistently, day after day.

### 7. Sunday Evening Post: Celebrate Your Progress

The Sunday Evening Post is a weekly summary automatically compiled by your AI Crew. It gives you a moment to step back, see everything that was accomplished, and feel your steady progress — something easy to miss during busy days.

Reading it each week leaves you feeling more grounded and clear, as you celebrate your wins and personal growth.

Because progress deserves to be recognized, not overlooked.
